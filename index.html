Standard schedule

Your normal shift per day. These hours are counted automatically every period. Leave both times blank for a day off.
Deducted from every shift's paid hours. Your shift's clock length minus this is what's paid.

Tillæg — per hour, always

Added to every worked hour on top of base (e.g. anciennitet, køle, lager).

Holddriftstillæg — night window

Extra per hour for time worked inside the window (e.g. 18:00–06:00). Your 05:00–06:00 hour counts automatically.

Overtime

Hours beyond the day's standard hours. Paid as a supplement on top of base, tiered like a Danish overenskomst.
